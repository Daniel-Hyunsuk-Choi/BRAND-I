Brand:I prioritizes the safety of all users, especially minors.

1. Overview

Brand:I is a challenge community platform for healthy habit formation, committed to providing a safe environment for users of all ages.

2. Zero-Tolerance Policy for Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Brand:I takes child safety seriously and enforces the following zero-tolerance policy:

  • Absolutely no Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) is permitted
  • All content that exploits or endangers children is prohibited
  • Such content is immediately removed upon discovery and reported to authorities
  • Violating users are permanently banned from the platform

3. User Reporting System

Users can easily report inappropriate content or behavior within the app:

  • "Report" button available on all posts and comments
  • Chat message reporting feature
  • Reported content reviewed within 24 hours
  • Serious matters addressed immediately
  • Reporter identity is protected

4. Content Monitoring and Safety Measures

4.1 Automated Detection System

  • AI-based content filtering
  • Automatic blocking of inappropriate keywords
  • Suspicious pattern detection

4.2 Manual Review

  • Regular monitoring by dedicated safety team
  • Priority review of reported content
  • Expert consultation when necessary

5. Reporting to Authorities

Upon discovering suspected child abuse cases or illegal content, we immediately report to:

Korean Authorities

  • National Police Agency Cyber Bureau (cyberbureau.police.go.kr)
  • Korea Internet & Security Agency (KISA) Illegal Spam Response Center
  • Korea Communications Standards Commission

International Organizations

  • NCMEC (National Center for Missing & Exploited Children, USA)
  • Law enforcement agencies in relevant countries

6. Additional Minor Protection Measures

  • Minors may use the service with parental consent
  • Enhanced privacy protection
  • Age-appropriate content filtering
  • Safe communication environment

7. Legal Compliance

Brand:I complies with all relevant laws and regulations, including:

  • Act on the Protection of Children and Youth from Sexual Abuse (South Korea)
  • Personal Information Protection Act
  • Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection
  • International child protection regulations and standards
